Knitting


Knitting is

  • Using interlocking loops to create fabric
  • A descriptor of the fabric during creation
  • A hobby of mine since 2001

Knitting used to be considered an activity for older ladies. Now it's more widespread and knitters can be found in every demographic. It can be as easy or difficult, as affordable or pricey as you want.

Sometimes I'd pick a fairly easy project and knit while walking. When that walking is done at San Diego Comic-Con, it can make for interesting photo opportunities.

Knitting Links

Knitter's Review is a great resource for knitters. The layout is well-defined and you can explore different areas of information from the top nav area.

Knitty is an online magazine featuring patterns and a few articles. It's a good way to get acquainted with up and coming designers. Their patterns have a fairly accurate difficulty rating system.

Ravelry is an online community of crafters. Users can track progress of their projects, keep record of their yarn stash, find patterns and chat with crafty people with similar interests. Love SQL and knitting? There's a forum for that.
